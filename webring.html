Julian he/him

    Chips fall like lost hope—
silent hands chase fading luck,
night swallows the day.

But the house always wins, no matter the play,
Takes your soul in a velvet way.
Bet it all on a fool’s parade,
Now I’m broke in more than just what I paid.
Yeah, the house always wins… and I stayed.
